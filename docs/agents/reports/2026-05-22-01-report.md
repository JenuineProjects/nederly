## 2. Researcher Findings

Key findings this run (§6 and §7 of `researcher.md`):

- **Professional app design:** Duolingo and Babbel both use white-surface cards, rounded shapes, high-contrast colour systems, and predictable navigation. Warm background + white card is the pattern that signals "trustworthy app" at a glance.
- **Phonetic display:** IPA is precise but inaccessible for beginners and dyslexic learners. Respelling (plain-language approximations) outperforms IPA for this profile. Oxford's accessible editions make the same choice.
- **Dyslexia + phonetics:** Multisensory delivery is essential — visual text + audio together. Colour-coding phoneme categories and consistent chip placement reduce cognitive load.
- **Flashcard layout:** The evidence-based pattern is: word (large) → phonetic hint (below, always visible) → audio button (always tappable) → meaning (post-reveal). Phonetic hint must sit *above* the reveal line.
- **Lesson notes summary:** Les 1 covered the Dutch sound system, home vocabulary (10 words), family vocabulary (11 words), and function words. Les 2 covered café vocabulary (30 words in 5 categories), a full dialogue, and 6 survival phrases. No phonetic hints were included in Les 2.

## 5. App Changes Made

Changes are grouped from `design_spec.md` §7.

### Data Model (changes 1–3)

- **9 new fields added to every word object** in `CATEGORIES`: `article`, `respelling`, `audioRequired`, `articulatoryHint`, `ipa`, `trapRank`, `soundCategory`, `syllableCount`, `phraseChunks`.
- **`initStorage()` updated** to seed all new fields when building word objects into `K.words`.
- **VERSION bumped** from `'5'` to `'6'` — triggers localStorage reseed on next open.

### CSS Tokens (change 4)

- **`:root` block added** at top of `<style>` with 20 custom properties: backgrounds (`--bg`, `--surface`, `--surface-raised`), text (`--text-primary`, `--text-secondary`), borders (`--border`, `--border-light`), and 5 accent colour pairs (blue, green, red, amber, purple) plus 8px spacing rhythm tokens.

### Session Screen — Flashcard Mode (changes 5, 9–16, 41–50)

- **Phonetic chip always visible** (pre- and post-reveal): purple chip (`#F0EBF9` background, `#5C3F8F` text) shows respelling below the Dutch word at all times.
- **★ badge on audio-required words**: tapping ★ toggles the articulatory hint paragraph (hidden by default, `#5C5C5C` text, 16px).
- **Slow button always rendered** — removed `revealed ?` conditional.
- **Divider `<hr>`** added between audio row and revealed content.
- **Article chip post-reveal**: `de` (blue chip, `#2E6DA4`) or `het` (green chip, `#2F7A3B`) shown inline before English meaning; `null` renders no chip.
- **Auto-play on first card** of a session (`sessionIndex === 0`).
- **Missed card re-insertion**: incorrect outcome splices the card back into `S.sessionWords` at `current + 2` before advancing.
- **`getHint()` and `HINTS` removed** — replaced by per-word `articulatoryHint` field.
- **`hintExpanded: {}`** added to initial `S` state; toggle-hint action handler added.

### Visual Polish — All Screens (changes 5–8, 17–40)

- **Background:** `#FAF3E0` → `#F7F2E8` (warmer cream, less yellow).
- **Header:** white surface (`#FFFFFF`), stronger font-weight (`700`), increased font-size (`22px`), subtle bottom shadow.
- **Cards (session, pair, sound, lesson):** white surface, `16px` border-radius, `box-shadow`, increased padding.
- **Buttons (play, slow, missed, got-it, start, home, nav):** taller touch targets (`56px` min-height), `12px` border-radius, consistent shadow treatment.
- **Missed button:** amber border (`#B35C00`) and tint (`#FDF3E6`) — not red.
- **Nav buttons:** white surface, `#D4CBBA` border, hover → purple tint (`#F0EBF9`).
- **Minimal pairs:** both pair words set to `#1A1A1A` (removed colour differentiation between word-a and word-b).
- **Sound guide:** star colour `#E07B39` → `#B35C00`; card background → white.
- **Stat boxes:** `12px` border-radius, white background added.
- **Progress dots:** gap `6px` → `8px`; done-dot green `#3A7D44` → `#2F7A3B`.
- **`theme-color` meta tag** updated to `#F7F2E8`.
